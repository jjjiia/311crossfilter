This map visualizes the day of week, time of day, temperature condition, government agency, and location of ######## service requests made to NYC 311 in the year 2013.

311 is the city's way of providing non-emergency services to its residents. Requests can vary from the infrastructural (road repair, traffic lights, electrical outages) to isolated incidents (heating, lead testing, graffiti removal and noise complaints), as long as they directly affect the everyday lives of citizens.
