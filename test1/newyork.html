311 is the city's way of providing non-emergency services to its residents. It is also a way through which we can understand the lives of residents through their needs. How the service is used reflects the rhythm of the city itself.

While agencies such as the NYPD responds to incidents across all zipcodes, the Taxi and Limosine Commision is more concentrated in lower Manhattan and the area of Brooklyn adjacent to it.
Blocked access and street lights dominate the complaints during the week. However complaints about music and party noise take over the weekends.
